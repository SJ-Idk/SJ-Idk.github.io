Kipo Oak

Kipo Oak is the titular main protagonist of Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts. Kipo is a teenage girl who later finds out that she is a mute hybrid after dreaming her late mother who was also a mother hybrid.

Wolf

Wolf is the deuteragonist of the Netflix original, Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts. She is a tough-as-nails surface traveler who worked alone until she met Kipo.

Mandu

Mandu is one of Kipo's companions. She's a four-eyed and six-legged mute boar that roamed alone in Las Vistas until Kipo found her.

Benson & Dave

Benson Mekler is a self-assured human scavenger who lives on the surface with his long time best friend Dave and offers to help Kipo find her way home.

Dave is a constantly metamorphosing bug mute. He is a surface scavenger along with his human partner Benson.

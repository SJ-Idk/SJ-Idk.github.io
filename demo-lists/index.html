How To Make Honey Muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl.
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk, and vanilla in a separate larger bowl.
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in 2 batches, mixing thoroughly.
  4. Distribute mixture to greased muffin tins.
  5. Bake for 16 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  6. Enjoy!
